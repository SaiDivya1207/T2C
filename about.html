About T2C

We're on a mission to transform waste management by making recycling rewarding, accessible, and impactful for communities everywhere.

Our Mission

At Trash2Cash, we believe that environmental sustainability and economic incentives can work hand in hand. Our platform bridges the gap between waste generators and recycling facilities, creating value for everyone involved.

We’re not just a waste collection service – we’re building a movement where every piece of recyclable material represents an opportunity to earn rewards while protecting our planet.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Environmental Stewardship

We’re committed to reducing waste and protecting our planet for future generations.

Community First

We build eco-conscious communities focused on collective action and positive change.

Transparency

We are open about our processes, impact metrics, and how we handle recyclables.

Social Impact

We create economic opportunities while solving real-world environmental issues.
